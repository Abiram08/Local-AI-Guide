### COMMON SCAMS & HOW TO AVOID

#### Taxi Meter Scam
**Scam**: Driver claims meter broken, charges 2-3x  
**Solution**: Use Uber/Ola or agree on price before starting  
**Fair Price**: Check GoanFlow app for route pricing

---

#### Restaurant Bill Padding
**Scam**: Extra items added to bill  
**Solution**: Check bill carefully, question extras  
**Fair Price**: Compare with GoanFlow pricing database

---

#### Beach Shack Overcharging
**Scam**: Different prices for tourists vs locals  
**Solution**: Ask for menu with prices, compare with GoanFlow  
**Fair Price**: GoanFlow shows fair prices for all items

---

#### Fake Police/Officials
**Scam**: Fake police demand "fines" for violations  
**Solution**: Ask for ID, call tourist police (1177)  
**Real Police**: Always have ID, give receipt for fines
