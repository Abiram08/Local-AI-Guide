## 6. MONSOON SAFETY (June-September)

### Swimming Risks During Monsoon
| Risk | Details | Mitigation |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Strong Currents** | Rip tides, undertow can pull swimmers | RED FLAG = NO SWIMMING. Check flag system daily. |
| **Rough Waves** | 8-12 feet waves, unpredictable patterns | Only experienced swimmers with guide |
| **Flash Floods** | Sudden water level rise in rivers/streams | Avoid wading through streams in hinterlands |
| **Marine Life** | Jellyfish, sea urchins more active | Wear full wetsuit if swimming |
| **Poor Visibility** | Murky water, visibility <5 feet | No diving, minimal beach activities |

### Safe Monsoon Activities (‚úÖ Recommended)
- Waterfall visits (Dudhsagar safest with guide)
- Mangrove kayaking (calm backwaters)
- Heritage site tours (indoor, no weather impact)
- Spice plantation walks
- Monsoon photography (landscapes beautiful)

### Monsoon Climate Risks
- **Landslides:** Ghats area, avoid heavy rain days
- **Humidity:** 90%+ ‚Üí heat exhaustion, dehydration
- **Mosquito-borne Illness:** Dengue, malaria uptick. Use repellent, long sleeves evening.

## 7. PETTY CRIME & SCAMS GUIDE

### Common Scams Targeting Tourists
| Scam | How It Works | Prevention |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Fake Taxi Drivers** | Unmarked autos claim to be registered taxis | Use Uber/Ola app only, verify license plates |
| **Restaurant Bill Overcharge** | Menu says ‚Çπ100, bill says ‚Çπ500 | Ask for itemized bill, check rates before ordering |
| **Drug Solicitation** | Dealer approach ("Cocaine? Hash?") offers substandard stuff | Ignore, report to police. Heavy penalties exist. |
| **Beach Shack Drinks Spiked** | Alcohol added to soft drinks without consent | Watch bartender make drink, sip slowly |
| **Hostel Theft** | Roommates steal from lockers | Use padlock, don't leave valuables unattended |
| **Fake Booking Confirmations** | Email looks real but reservation not made | Book through Booking.com/Hostelworld, not random sites |
| **"Special Discount" Offers** | Tout claims exclusive deal, overcharges later | No upfront promises, prices fixed in advance |

### What to Do If Robbed
1. **Don't Resist:** Petty thieves avoid confrontation
2. **Report to Police:** Goa Police Helpline: 100
3. **Contact Embassy:** If travel docs stolen. US: +91-832-2438000, UK: +91-832-2440221
4. **Document Loss:** File police report for insurance claims

- **Helmet Mandatory:** ‚Çπ5,000-‚Çπ10,000 fine if caught without
- **License Required:** International Driving Permit (IDP) + passport
- **Drunk Driving:** Severe penalties, police checkpoints active weekends
- **Road Conditions:** Potholes, cows common. Slow down especially at night.
- **Insurance:** Rental bike insurance ‚Çπ50-100/day (optional but recommended)

### Auto-Rickshaw Safety
- **Use Meter:** Insist on meter or agree price before boarding
- **Avoid Solo at Night:** Use registered taxi apps
- **Valuable Theft:** Keep bags visible, not on seat
- **Route Knowledge:** Know route before boarding to avoid detours

### Taxi/Uber Safety
- **Verify Driver:** Check name, car type before boarding
- **Share Trip Details:** Give ride info to friend/hotel
- **Avoid Cash Rides:** Use app-based payment
- **Report Unsafe Drivers:** 1-star rating + report to Uber

## 10. VALUABLES & DOCUMENTATION SAFETY

### What NOT to Carry Daily
- All cash (keep bulk at hotel)
- Original passport (carry copy)
- Credit cards (1-2 maximum)
- Expensive jewelry
- Irreplaceable items

### Safe Storage
- **Hotel Safe:** Ask hotel for safe box (usually free)
- **Money Belt:** Under shirt, hidden
- **Travel Insurance:** Critical. Get comprehensive policy.
- **Photocopies:** Store copies of passport, visa, cards separately

### Lost/Stolen Document Procedures
- **Passport Lost:** Contact nearest embassy immediately
- **Card Fraud:** Call bank 24/7 hotline, request block
- **Insurance Claim:** Report within 24h to police
