## 7. TIPS FOR AVOIDING TOURIST TRAPS

1. **Eat Where Locals Eat:** Local eateries 40-60% cheaper
2. **Prepay Taxis:** Use Uber/Ola to avoid auto negotiations
3. **Buy Alcohol From Shops:** Not beach shacks (+200-300% markup)
4. **Rent for Longer Periods:** 7-day discounts save 30-40%
5. **Visit on Weekdays:** 30-50% fewer crowds, lower prices
6. **Negotiate Accommodation:** Peak season offers have room for negotiation
7. **Avoid Peak Hours:** 7-9 AM cheapest restaurant times
8. **Book Activities in Advance:** Online booking saves 10-15%

## 8. CURRENCY & PAYMENT NOTES

- **Primary Currency:** Indian Rupee (₹)
- **Exchange Rate (as of Dec 2025):** ₹104-106 per GBP, ₹83-85 per USD
- **Payment Methods:** UPI/Card accepted 70%, cash still required 30% (small shops, street vendors)
- **ATM:** Abundant, withdrawal fees ₹20-30
- **Digital Wallets:** Google Pay, Paytm, PhonePe widespread
- **Tipping:** 5-10% at restaurants (not mandatory), ₹20-50 for services
